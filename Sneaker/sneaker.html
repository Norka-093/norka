Our History

During the process of growing up, there are always some things that accompany our lives. For me, sneakers are one of those things. They are not only a necessary item for daily outings but also a fashionable piece. A simple pair of sneakers has become the starting point of our journey.

My connection with sneakers must mention the Nike Zoom Kobe Venomenon II endorsed by Kobe Bryant. Its stylish design, first-class performance, and comfort remind people of the golden age of basketball. Another companion is the Adidas Alphabounce 3 Aramis. It has accompanied me for many years. It can fit my feet comfortably, as if it were tailor-made for me.

As time goes by, my love for sneakers has grown stronger. Now, through this website, I aim to share my wearing experiences, hoping to inspire others to find the pair of sneakers that suits them best on the path of choosing sneakers.
