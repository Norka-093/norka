Production process

Step 1

Design & Planning

Designers create the blueprint for shoe models based on target requirements, market trends, and fashion fads. This blueprint covers dimensions, shape, color, materials, and functionality.

Step 2

Material Selection

The choice of materials directly impacts the performance and durability of sports shoes. A variety of materials are selected, including rubber, synthetic materials, fabrics, and memory foam.

Step 3

Component Assembly

Prepare each component (such as shoe uppers, laces, soles, etc.), test the compatibility and stability between components, and integrate all components to form a complete pair of sports shoes.

Step 4

Sales & Service

Sales and service are the final key points. Understand market demands and preferences to set the selling price, choose suitable sales channels, and provide customer service to enhance the brand image.

Our History

As we grow up, some things always stay with us. Sneakers are one such thing. They are essential for daily outings, fashion items, and the starting point of every journey.

My fate with sneakers started with the Nike Zoom Kobe Venomenon II endorsed by Kobe Bryant. Its stylish design and outstanding performance harken back to the golden age of basketball. As my long-term companion, the Adidas Alphabounce 3 Aramis is a perfect fit for me. As time went by, my love for sneakers grew.

Through this website, I share my sneaker experiences in the hopes of helping others find their ideal sneakers.
